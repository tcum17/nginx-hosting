Hobbies and Interests

Here are some of my favorite things to do and some things that I am interested in.

Reading

I love to read! However, I don't think I read the same things as most people. I spend a lot of time reading academic articles about various subject such as: Bioinformatics, Botany, Nature-Inspired Algorithms, and Neural Networks. I am also very fond of history. I consider myself to be a history buff. I am mainly interested in the ancient era religion -specifically pagan religions and practices.

Gaming

I spend a lot of time playing games. I have been playing games since I was a little kid, starting with a gameboy and a gamecube. Today, I have been mostly playing MOBAs and strategy games. I have always used video games as a way to connect with friends and have used them as a way to stay in contact with them during the pandemic.

Hiking

I love to go out into nature. It has always been a safe space for me. I am always relaxed and there is something so grounding about being out and away from civilization. Hiking allows me to not only get some good exercise in but also get some awesome views. I have hiked in Alaska, Colorado Utah, Arizona, and in various national parks.
